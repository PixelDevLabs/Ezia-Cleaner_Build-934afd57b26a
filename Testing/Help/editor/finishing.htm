Finishing Up

Once you have gotten your level to a certain point, you may want to start testing.   To play test your level, you can hit the Play button in the Realms box or just press 'p'.  When you are done testing, press 'Esc' to stop and go back to the Editor.

If you need to delete something, select that item and hit the 'Del' key.

It is a good idea to save often while you are designing and play testing your level.

Want to show your level off?  You will notice that your finished Postal level is quite small and easy to send via e-mail. There are many Postal Fan sites that would love to host your level. Check around at http://www.gopostal.com for more information on the Postal Fan Sites.
