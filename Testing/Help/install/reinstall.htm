Re-Installing Postal Plus

Under certain circumstances, it may be necessary to re-install the game.

If so, follow these steps:

  1. Insert the Postal Plus CD.

  2. If you are prompted to start Postal Plus, click No or Cancel.

  3. Click the Windows Start button, then click Run.

  4. Type the following and then click OK:
     
        D:\setup
     
    where D is the letter of your CD-ROM drive.

  5. This starts Setup.   Follow the on-screen instructions.
