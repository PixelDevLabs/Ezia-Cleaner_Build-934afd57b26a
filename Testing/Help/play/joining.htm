Joining a Multiplayer Game

You can join a game by selecting START then MULTIPLAYER and then JOIN GAME.

There are two ways to join:

  • BROWSE:  Select this if you are playing on a Local Area Network (LAN).  The screen will list all the games that are starting on your network.  Simply click on the one you want to join.

  • CONNECT TO:  Use this if you are playing on the Internet or your LAN does not support browsing.  You will need to enter the address of the computer that is hosting the game into the space provided, and then press Enter.

    The host player must communicate his computer's address to all the other players before the game begins.  This must be done verbally, by phone, via email or some other similar method.
